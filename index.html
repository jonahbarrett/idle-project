Kingdom Creator v0.2

Welcome to Kingdom Creator! Starting with 10 gold, build your own empire.
Use your 10 gold to hire a lumberjack.

Farmers

Farmers will gather food for you.

You have 0 farmers, they gather food/sec.

Hiring a farmer will cost 10 gold and 1 worker.

Lumberjacks

Lumberjacks will chop down trees to gather wood for you.

You have 0 lumberjacks, they gather wood/sec.

Hiring a lumberjack will cost 10 gold and 1 worker.

Quarriers

Quarriers will mine stone for you.

Iron Miners

Iron Miners will mine iron for you.

Workers

Hire workers and assign them to different jobs.

You have 0 workers.

Hiring a worker will cost 10 gold and 5 food.

Hire A Merchant!

Merchants will sell some of your resources for you every few seconds, and for more gold than you can.

You have 0 merchants, they will sell 0 of your wood every 10 seconds.

Your next merchant will cost 150 gold to hire.


Sell Your Resources

You have wood.

Sell your wood for 1 gold per 2 wood.

Selling all your wood will give you gold.


Land

You will be able to purchase land here.


Disclaimer: This game is unfinished and constantly being updated. If your game is not working you will have to delete your save in the options tab.
